The Ultimate Cheeseburger

Cheese, crispy bacon, pickled onion, spicy mayo…

a good burger with all the trimmings really is hard to beat and this one has it all!

Ingredients

  • 500g beef mince
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp ketchup
  • 1 tsp thyme leaves, finely chopped
  • Sea salt and cracked black pepper
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Pickled red onion;
  • 1 red onion, very thinly sliced
  • Juice of 2 lemons
  • 75g mayonnaise
  • 1-2 tbsp hot sauce
  • 4 seeded buns
  • 8 slices pancetta
  • 75g gruyere cheese, sliced
  • 50g rocket

Steps

  1. Put the red onion and lemon juice into a small zip lock bag ensuring all the onions are immersed in the liquid. Leave aside to pickle for at least 1 hour.
  2. For the burger, combine the minced beef, Dijon, Worcestershire sauce, garlic, ketchup, thyme, salt and pepper in a large bowl and mix until everything is evenly combined. Divide into 4 and shape into patties. Place a large pan on a medium high heat and add the 1 tbsp of oil.
  3. Fry the streaky bacon for 2-3 minutes on either side or until nice and crisp, set aside. Reduce the heat to medium and place the burger patties in the pan, fry for 6 minutes on either side or until cooked through and browned. Once cooked, top with the cheese and cover with a cloche or lid for 3-4 minutes until the cheese is oozing and melted.
  4. Place the bottom of the toasted bun onto a plate, smear with the spicy mayo, top with the burger, crisp streaky bacon, pickled red onion and rocket followed by the burger bun top.
